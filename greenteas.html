Green Teas

Green tea is a popular beverage made from the leaves of the Camellia sinensis plant, known for its antioxidants and potential health benefits. This results in a light, refreshing flavor and a slightly greenish color.

Gentle Energy

Green tea is also known for its relatively low caffeine content compared to other types of tea, making it a popular choice for those seeking a milder stimulant.

A Variety of teas

Green tea encompasses a diverse range of varieties and aromas (from most common to least) including:

    Types

  • Sencha
  • Matcha
  • Gyokuro
  • Jasmine

    Aromas

  1. Grassy
  2. Earthy
  3. Floral
  4. Nutty

Zen Brews

In Buddhism, green tea is revered not just as a beverage, but as a symbol of mindfulness and harmony with nature.
